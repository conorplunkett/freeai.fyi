FAQ

Questions advertisers ask.

Where do my ads show?

On the sponsored line that appears while Claude, ChatGPT and Gemini think — in the Chrome extension, in the terminal beside Claude Code, and above the composer in the native Mac apps. It's the one moment every AI user is staring at the screen.

How does pricing work?

It's a live CPM auction. You set a budget and a CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions) and receive budget × 1,000 ÷ CPM impressions. The highest CPM serves first — outbid the current top to take the #1 slot, or bid the floor to join the queue.

Am I billed per impression or per click?

Per impression — clicks are a bonus. Your receipt breaks out both: a CPM report of where the ad was seen and a CPC report of where the clicks came from.

How do I pay?

Checkout runs through Stripe. Fill in the form, hit Pay with Stripe, and you're redirected to a secure Stripe Checkout page. Your campaign goes live once payment clears.

Can I run a recurring or always-on budget?

The self-serve form funds a single campaign. For a recurring monthly budget, managed pacing, or a guaranteed top slot, email ads@contact.freeai.fyi and we'll set it up.

What's the inventory, and can I target it?

Inventory is every "thinking" moment across Claude, ChatGPT and Gemini — in the browser, the CLI and the desktop. Today you control budget, CPM (placement priority) and creative (line, color, link); finer surface and audience targeting is on the roadmap — ask us if you need it now.
