Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

FreeAI turns the loading spinner in ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini into a tiny ad marketplace and returns 50% of the revenue to you as credits redeemable for Claude. This policy explains the little bit of data we need to do that, and what we deliberately never touch.

What we never collect

  • Your code. The extension does not read, transmit, or store the contents of your files, prompts, or agent output.
  • Your keystrokes. We don't log what you type into your editor or terminal.
  • Your projects. Repository names, paths, and contents stay on your machine.

What we collect

  • Aggregate impression & click counts — to know which sponsored line served, when, and how much you earned.
  • Payout details — handled by Stripe so we can pay you. We never see your full bank details.
  • Basic device info — editor type and version, to render the ad line correctly.

Who we share with

  • Stripe — payment and payout processing.
  • Google Cloud — hosting, authentication, and storage of the counts above.

That's it. We don't sell your data, because the only data we have is "a spinner showed an ad."

Your choices

You can opt out of any ad category, pause FreeAI, or uninstall at any time — your earnings to date are still paid out. Questions? Email privacy@freeai.fyi.
