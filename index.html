Why trust the 90%?

Because you can audit it.

Our backend is open source — the auction, the ledger, the payout sweep, all of it. The other guys keep their auction engine private and pay “up to 50%”. We publish the code that computes your cut.

  • Append-only ledger. Your balance is never a number we edit — it's the sum of every credit and debit, recomputed from the ledger on every read. Balances can't drift.
  • Millicent math. Amounts are stored in 1/1000ths of a cent so the 90/10 split is exact, not rounded in our favor.
  • Server-side clicks. Clicks settle through single-use tracking tokens — advertisers aren't billed for forged clicks, and your click credits are provable.
  • Anonymous from day one. No sign-in to start earning. Email is only needed when you cash out, and payouts run through Stripe — we never touch your bank details.
